UK Political Accountability Dashboard — Did They Deliver?

Did They Deliver is an independent, politically neutral tracker holding UK Prime Ministers to account on their key election pledges. We track promises made by every Prime Minister since 1997 — from Tony Blair to Keir Starmer — against verifiable public data from the ONS, NHS England, Parliament.uk and other official sources.

What We Track

  • NHS waiting list — 7.10 million patients waiting (down from 7.54M peak). Has any PM hit the 18-week target?
  • Net migration — Cameron's "tens of thousands" pledge vs 204,000/yr today
  • CPI inflation — From 11.1% peak in 2022 to 2.8% in 2026
  • House prices — £285,000 average. Up 63% since 2010
  • PM pledge tracker — Every broken and kept promise, by party

Current UK Political Situation

The 2024 general election delivered a historic Labour majority of 412 seats under Sir Keir Starmer. The Conservative Party holds 121 seats. Our live map shows the full 2024 results by UK constituency. Use the MP finder to look up your local representative by postcode.
